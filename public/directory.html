Recovery Directory

Help for the man who wants to quit drinking.

A curated list of the tools other men have leaned on. Apps that count the days, books that change the way you see it, meditation, community, planners, and even the shirt on your back. Filter by what you need. Every link goes straight to the source.

This is the Recovery Directory, the first of several planned for Crown and Compass. It is a starting point, not a treatment plan. If you are in crisis, call or text 988.
