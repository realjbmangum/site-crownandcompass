Reading Guide

Stepping Up

Dennis Rainey

A simple guideBeingBalance
Get the book

What it is about

Rainey maps a man's life through five stages: boyhood, adolescence, manhood, mentor, and patriarch. He shows what it looks like to step up to courageous manhood at each one, and what happens to a man, and to everyone around him, when he refuses.

It is a plain, direct call to stop drifting and take up the responsibility God set in front of you.

The big idea

Manhood is not a matter of age. It is a series of moments where a man chooses courage over comfort and steps up to what God has given him to carry.

Talk it through

  1. Of Rainey's five stages, which best describes where you are right now, and why?
  2. Where have you been stuck in a stage longer than you should be?
  3. What does stepping up look like this week inside your own home?
  4. Who stepped up for you, and who is watching you now?
  5. Where are you tempted to stay a boy when a man is needed?
  6. What responsibility have you been avoiding that God is asking you to carry?
  7. Who could you begin to mentor, even a little, in this next season?

Read the Word

1 Corinthians 16:13-14 Joshua 1:9 1 Kings 2:2-3 Deuteronomy 6:6-7 1 Timothy 4:12

One practice

Pick the one area where you have been avoiding stepping up. Take one specific action this week to carry that responsibility, and ask a brother to check in with you about it.
