The Daily Tracker.

Four things, every day. The reading, the workout, the prayer, and one honest word to someone who matters. Each one is small. Together they hold a life on its bearing. Print this page, pin it where the morning will find you, and fill a box each day you show up. A week is short. See what a week can carry.
