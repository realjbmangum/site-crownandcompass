Coming in a later wave

Not built yet. Here is the plan.

We are shipping the reading and the chapters first, because that is the point. Community comes next, and only when we can do it right. This page is the plan, not a promise of dates. When it lands it will serve your chapter, not the whole internet.

What it will be

Quiet, and yours

  • Your chapter only. The men you meet with, not strangers.
  • Short, honest notes. A line about your week, not a highlight reel.
  • Prayer you can carry. Requests from your men, in one place.
  • Read once, done. No reason to keep checking it all day.

What it will not be

Not a social network

  • No likes, no reactions, no scores to chase.
  • No follower counts. A man is not a number here.
  • No endless feed built to keep you scrolling.
  • No performing. Show up honest or stay quiet.
