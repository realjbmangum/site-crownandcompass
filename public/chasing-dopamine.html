I wasted yesterday chasing dopamine

By BrianNovember 23, 20252 min read

Found Grok AI video generation. Lost my mind creating content. Hours disappeared. Now I'm empty.

You know this feeling. One more video. One more scroll. One more trade. One more project.

It was an easy day. I ate like a king. Low activity. Perfect storm for distraction.

I asked God for hope and for a lesson in it all. Here's what I'm learning: dopamine hits are pause buttons. They don't solve problems. They delay them.

I need to be strong for my family and my brothers. Bari comes back today and I need to be present.

What's your dopamine trap?

  • Social media
  • Trading apps
  • YouTube rabbit holes
  • Porn
  • Food

Pick one. Abstain for 7 days.

Discipline is freedom.

Brian
