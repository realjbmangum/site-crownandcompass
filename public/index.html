How It Works

Three things. Done well, together.

I.

Read

One book every six weeks. Read it slowly, mark it up, wrestle with it. Iron takes time to sharpen.
II.

Share Life

The book is the doorway; the men are the point. We show up honest, the wins, the failures, the fight.
III.

Meet

When the last page turns, we gather to close it out, and set the bearing for the next leg.

The Rhythm

One book. Six weeks. Repeat.

The cadence is the discipline. Every cycle runs the same arc: pick it up together, walk it out, and gather to close it before the next begins.

WK 01

Kickoff

Book announced. Bearing set.

WK 02

Read

Dig in. Notes in the thread.

WK 03

Check In

Mid-book. Life on the table.

WK 04

Read

Push through the middle.

WK 05

Finish

Last pages. Land the plane.

WK 06

Gather

Meet, close it out, reset.

What We Read

Books that don't let you stay the same.

We choose books with weight, on faith, character, calling, and the long obedience of following Christ. No fluff, no hype cycle. If it won't sharpen a man, it doesn't make the list.

Every six weeks the brotherhood votes on what's next, so the shelf stays yours.
