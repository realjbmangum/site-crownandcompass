Take up the next book.

One book every six weeks, read together, online and worldwide. Leave your name and we will send you the next cycle's book and how to join. No application, no fee, no commitment for the first visit.

What happens next

  1. You fill this out

    Name, email, how you heard, and a sentence or two if you want. It takes about a minute. Nothing else is required.

  2. You hear back within a day or two

    A real person reads every message and writes back. Not an autoresponder, not a sales sequence. A real reply from a real man.

  3. You're in the next cycle

    When the next six-week cycle opens, we send you the book and walk you in. You read it with the brotherhood, and we gather to close it out before the next one begins.

It's free.

No fee, no application, no membership. Crown and Compass is free because the Gospel is free, and because the best brotherhoods have never charged for belonging. You are not paying to be here. You belong because you show up.
